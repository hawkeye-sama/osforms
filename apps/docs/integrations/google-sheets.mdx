title: "Google Sheets"

---

## Setup

1. Go to **Integrations** in your osforms dashboard
2. Click **Connect** next to Google Sheets
3. Sign in with your Google account and grant access
4. Paste your Google Sheet URL or ID
5. Optionally specify a sheet name (defaults to the first sheet)
6. Save

## How columns work

osforms automatically detects your sheet's header row and maps submission fields to the correct columns. If a field doesn't have a matching column, it's added to the end of the header row automatically.

Two system columns are always added:

| Column | Value |
|---|---|
| `Submission ID` | MongoDB ObjectId of the submission |
| `Submitted At` | ISO 8601 timestamp |

## Field key mapping

Column headers match the **field key** set in your form builder (e.g. `full_name`, `email`, `message`). You can customize field keys in the form builder under each field's settings.

<Note>
  The Google account you connect must have edit access to the spreadsheet.
</Note>
